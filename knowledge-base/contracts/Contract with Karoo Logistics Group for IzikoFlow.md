# Contract with Karoo Logistics Group for IzikoFlow

**Contract Number:** IZ-FLW-2024-B-0012
**Effective Date:** 1 May 2024
**Initial Term:** 24 months
**Renewal:** Auto-renews for 12 months unless cancelled with 60 days' notice

## Parties

- **Service Provider:** Iziko Cloud Solutions (Pty) Ltd
- **Customer:** Karoo Logistics Group (Pty) Ltd ("Karoo Logistics")

## Service Description

Iziko shall provide Karoo Logistics with the **IzikoFlow Business Tier** low-code workflow automation platform, supporting up to 50 active workflows and 10,000 monthly runs. The deployment is focused on driver-onboarding, vehicle-inspection, and incident-reporting workflows.

## Commercials

- **Monthly fee:** R 32,000 (ex VAT)
- **Total contract value:** R 768,000 over the 24-month initial term
- **Once-off onboarding fee:** R 40,000
- **Annual escalation:** CPI + 1.5%

## Service Levels

- 99.5% monthly platform uptime
- 24x5 support with 1-hour response on Severity 1 incidents

## Compliance

- POPIA compliance with signed data-processing addendum
- 7-year audit trail retention for all driver-onboarding workflows
