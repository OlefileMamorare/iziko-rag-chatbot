# Contract with Pioneer Retail South Africa for IzikoConnect

**Contract Number:** IZ-CON-2024-S-0017
**Effective Date:** 1 June 2024
**Initial Term:** 36 months
**Renewal:** Auto-renews for 12 months unless cancelled with 90 days' notice

## Parties

- **Service Provider:** Iziko Cloud Solutions (Pty) Ltd
- **Customer:** Pioneer Retail South Africa (Pty) Ltd ("Pioneer Retail")

## Service Description

Iziko shall provide Pioneer Retail with the **IzikoConnect Standard Tier** unified cloud collaboration platform for up to 500 named head-office and regional-office users. (Store users are covered under a separate, store-level licence not included in this contract.)

## Commercials

- **Monthly fee:** R 45,000 (ex VAT)
- **Total contract value:** R 1,620,000 over the 36-month initial term
- **Once-off onboarding fee:** R 60,000
- **Annual escalation:** CPI + 2%

## Service Levels

- 99.9% monthly platform uptime
- 24x5 standard support
- Priority response of 30 minutes for Severity 1 incidents

## Compliance

- POPIA compliance with signed data-processing addendum
- Shared single sign-on with Pioneer Retail's existing Microsoft Entra ID tenant

## Signatories

- For Iziko: **Thandiwe Mokoena**, CEO
- For Pioneer Retail: Mr. S. Pillay, Group Chief Data Officer
