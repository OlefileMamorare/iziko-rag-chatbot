# Contract with Western Cape Health Network for IzikoGuard

**Contract Number:** IZ-GRD-2024-A-0022
**Effective Date:** 1 April 2024
**Initial Term:** 24 months
**Renewal:** Auto-renews for 12 months unless cancelled with 90 days' notice

## Parties

- **Service Provider:** Iziko Cloud Solutions (Pty) Ltd
- **Customer:** Western Cape Health Network NPC ("WCHN"), a non-profit network of 22 private clinics across the Western Cape

## Service Description

Iziko shall provide WCHN with the **IzikoGuard Advanced Tier** managed cybersecurity service across up to 1,000 monitored endpoints. The service includes 24x7 SOC monitoring and 8 incident-response hours per month.

## Commercials

- **Monthly fee:** R 78,000 (ex VAT)
- **Total contract value:** R 1,872,000 over the 24-month initial term
- **Once-off onboarding fee:** R 90,000
- **Annual escalation:** CPI + 1.5%

## Service Levels

- 24x7 SOC monitoring with 30-minute acknowledgement for critical alerts
- Quarterly compliance attestations covering POPIA and the National Health Act

## Compliance

- POPIA compliance with signed data-processing addendum
- Healthcare-specific incident-response runbook (Annex C)

- For Iziko: **Thandiwe Mokoena**, CEO
- For WCHN: Dr. L. de Villiers, Executive Director
