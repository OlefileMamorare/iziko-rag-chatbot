# Contract with Sandton Capital Partners for IzikoFlow

**Contract Number:** IZ-FLW-2024-E-0009
**Effective Date:** 1 February 2024
**Initial Term:** 36 months
**Renewal:** Auto-renews for 12 months unless cancelled with 120 days' notice

## Parties

- **Service Provider:** Iziko Cloud Solutions (Pty) Ltd
- **Customer:** Sandton Capital Partners (Pty) Ltd ("Sandton Capital"), a Sandton-based asset manager managing R 28 billion in client assets

## Service Description

Iziko shall provide Sandton Capital with the **IzikoFlow Enterprise Tier** low-code workflow automation platform, with unlimited workflows and runs and a dedicated solutions architect.

- **Monthly fee:** R 75,000 (ex VAT)
- **Total contract value:** R 2,700,000 over the 36-month initial term
- **Once-off onboarding fee:** R 95,000
- **Annual escalation:** CPI + 2%

## Service Levels

- 99.9% monthly platform uptime
- 24x7 support with 30-minute response on Severity 1 incidents
- Quarterly architecture review with the dedicated solutions architect

## Compliance

- FAIS-aligned audit trail retention of 7 years for all approval workflows
