# Contract with Cape Town Mutual Bank for IzikoConnect

**Contract Number:** IZ-CON-2024-S-0011
**Effective Date:** 1 March 2024
**Initial Term:** 36 months
**Renewal:** Auto-renews for 12 months unless cancelled with 90 days' notice

## Parties

- **Service Provider:** Iziko Cloud Solutions (Pty) Ltd ("Iziko")
- **Customer:** Cape Town Mutual Bank ("CTMB"), a registered mutual bank with head office in Cape Town

## Service Description

Iziko shall provide CTMB with the **IzikoConnect Standard Tier** unified cloud collaboration platform for up to 500 named users. The deployment is hosted in Iziko's Cape Town region with data residency in the Republic of South Africa.

## Commercials

- **Monthly fee:** R 45,000 (ex VAT), billed monthly in arrears
- **Total contract value:** R 1,620,000 over the 36-month initial term
- **Once-off onboarding fee:** R 60,000 (waived in this contract)
- **Annual escalation:** CPI + 2%, applied on each anniversary date

## Service Levels

- 99.9% monthly platform uptime, measured against the production service status page
- 24x5 standard support, with service credits payable if SLA is missed
- Priority response of 30 minutes for Severity 1 incidents

## Compliance

- POPIA compliance with documented data-processing addendum (Annex B)
- ISO 27001 statement of applicability provided annually
- Quarterly access-review reports delivered to CTMB's CISO

## Signatories

- For Iziko: **Thandiwe Mokoena**, CEO
- For CTMB: Mr. J. Adams, Group Chief Operating Officer
