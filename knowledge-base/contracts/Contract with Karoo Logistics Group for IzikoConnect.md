# Contract with Karoo Logistics Group for IzikoConnect

**Contract Number:** IZ-CON-2023-E-0007
**Effective Date:** 1 July 2023
**Initial Term:** 24 months
**Renewal:** Auto-renews for 12 months unless cancelled with 90 days' notice

## Parties

- **Service Provider:** Iziko Cloud Solutions (Pty) Ltd
- **Customer:** Karoo Logistics Group (Pty) Ltd ("Karoo Logistics"), a national logistics operator headquartered in Bloemfontein

## Service Description

Iziko shall provide Karoo Logistics with the **IzikoConnect Enterprise Tier** for unlimited users across the customer's 14 depots and head office. The service includes 24x7 support and a dedicated customer success manager.

## Commercials

- **Monthly fee:** R 95,000 (ex VAT)
- **Total contract value:** R 2,280,000 over the 24-month initial term
- **Once-off onboarding fee:** R 120,000
- **Annual escalation:** CPI + 1.5%

## Service Levels

- 99.9% monthly platform uptime
- 24x7 support with a dedicated CSM (currently **Pieter Coetzee**'s team)
- Priority response of 15 minutes for Severity 1 incidents

## Compliance

- POPIA compliance with signed data-processing addendum
- Annual third-party penetration test report shared under NDA
