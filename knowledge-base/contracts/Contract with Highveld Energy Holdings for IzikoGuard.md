# Contract with Highveld Energy Holdings for IzikoGuard

**Contract Number:** IZ-GRD-2024-E-0019
**Effective Date:** 1 January 2024
**Initial Term:** 36 months
**Renewal:** Auto-renews for 12 months unless cancelled with 120 days' notice

## Parties

- **Service Provider:** Iziko Cloud Solutions (Pty) Ltd
- **Customer:** Highveld Energy Holdings Ltd ("Highveld Energy"), a JSE-listed independent power producer headquartered in Sandton

## Service Description

Iziko shall provide Highveld Energy with the **IzikoGuard Enterprise Tier** managed cybersecurity service across an unlimited number of monitored endpoints. The service includes 24x7 SOC monitoring, 24 incident-response hours per month, and a dedicated security advisor.

## Commercials

- **Monthly fee:** R 165,000 (ex VAT)
- **Total contract value:** R 5,940,000 over the 36-month initial term
- **Once-off onboarding fee:** R 240,000
- **Annual escalation:** CPI + 2%

## Service Levels

- 24x7 SOC monitoring with 15-minute acknowledgement for critical alerts
- Quarterly tabletop incident-response exercises
- Monthly executive threat briefing delivered to Highveld Energy's CISO

## Compliance

- POPIA compliance with signed data-processing addendum
- ISO 27001 statement of applicability provided annually
- Quarterly POPIA breach-readiness review
