# Contract with Pioneer Retail South Africa for IzikoData

**Contract Number:** IZ-DAT-2023-G-0014
**Effective Date:** 1 September 2023
**Initial Term:** 24 months
**Renewal:** Auto-renews for 12 months unless cancelled with 90 days' notice

## Parties

- **Service Provider:** Iziko Cloud Solutions (Pty) Ltd
- **Customer:** Pioneer Retail South Africa (Pty) Ltd ("Pioneer Retail"), a national speciality retailer with 340 stores

## Service Description

Iziko shall provide Pioneer Retail with the **IzikoData Growth Tier** managed analytics platform, including 5 TB of managed warehouse storage, unlimited connectors, and up to 100 analytics users.

## Commercials

- **Monthly fee:** R 88,000 (ex VAT)
- **Total contract value:** R 2,112,000 over the 24-month initial term
- **Once-off onboarding fee:** R 110,000
- **Annual escalation:** CPI + 2%

## Service Levels

- 99.5% monthly platform uptime
- 24x5 support with 1-hour response on Severity 1 incidents
- Quarterly business review with the Iziko data architect

## Compliance

- POPIA compliance with signed data-processing addendum
- Annual data classification review

- For Pioneer Retail: Mr. S. Pillay, Group Chief Data Officer
