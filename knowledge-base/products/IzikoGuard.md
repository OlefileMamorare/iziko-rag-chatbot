# IzikoGuard

## Summary

IzikoGuard is Iziko Cloud Solutions' managed cybersecurity service. It combines a 24x7 Security Operations Centre (SOC) based in Cape Town with a proprietary threat-detection engine that ingests logs from cloud workloads, endpoints, and network appliances.

## Target Customers

Organisations subject to South African regulatory requirements (POPIA, FAIS, the Cybercrimes Act) that need around-the-clock monitoring without operating their own SOC. Especially strong fit for financial services, healthcare, and energy & utilities.

## Key Features

- 24x7 SOC with local analysts (Cape Town and Johannesburg)
- Proprietary IzikoGuard Detection Engine (developed in-house by **Sipho Dlamini**, winner of the 2023 SATIOTY award)
- Integration with all major SIEMs, EDR vendors, and cloud providers (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- Phishing simulation and security-awareness training included in the Advanced Tier and above
- Incident response retainer hours included from the Advanced Tier (8 hours per month) and Enterprise Tier (24 hours per month)
- POPIA breach-notification workflow templates and Information Regulator liaison support

## Pricing Tiers (per month, ex VAT, billed in ZAR)

- **Essential Tier** - R 35,000 per month. Up to 250 monitored endpoints, 8x5 SOC, monthly threat report.
- **Advanced Tier** - R 78,000 per month. Up to 1,000 monitored endpoints, 24x7 SOC, 8 incident response hours per month.
- **Enterprise Tier** - R 165,000 per month. Unlimited endpoints, 24x7 SOC, 24 incident response hours per month, dedicated security advisor.
