# IzikoFlow

## Summary

IzikoFlow is a low-code workflow automation platform that lets business teams build, deploy, and govern internal workflows without engineering involvement. It is positioned as the local, POPIA-compliant alternative to global low-code tools.

## Target Customers

Operations, finance, HR, and customer-service teams in mid-market and enterprise organisations that need to automate internal processes (employee onboarding, vendor approvals, claims triage, customer escalations).

## Key Features

- Visual drag-and-drop workflow designer
- 80+ pre-built connectors including South African favourites (Sage, Pastel, Discovery Health, FNB Business)
- Forms with conditional logic and POPIA-compliant consent fields
- Audit log of every workflow run, retained for 7 years
- Approval flows via email, WhatsApp Business, or in-app

## Pricing Tiers (per month, ex VAT, billed in ZAR)

- **Starter Tier** - R 12,000 per month. Up to 10 active workflows, 1,000 runs per month.
- **Business Tier** - R 32,000 per month. Up to 50 active workflows, 10,000 runs per month.
- **Enterprise Tier** - R 75,000 per month. Unlimited workflows and runs, dedicated solutions architect.
