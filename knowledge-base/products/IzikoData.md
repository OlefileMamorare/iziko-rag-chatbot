# IzikoData

## Summary

IzikoData is a managed data analytics and business intelligence platform built on top of open-source engines (DuckDB, Trino, and dbt) with managed pipelines, governance, and a self-service BI front-end. All data is stored in South African regions and never leaves the country.

## Target Customers

Data-driven mid-market and enterprise customers who want modern analytics without building a large in-house data team. Strong fit for retail, telecommunications, and financial services.

## Key Features

- Managed connectors for common South African business systems (Sage, SAP S/4HANA, Salesforce, HubSpot, Xero, Pastel)
- Drag-and-drop dashboards with row-level security
- Embedded notebooks for analysts (SQL and Python)
- Data lineage and POPIA-aware data classification
- Up to 5 TB of managed warehouse storage included on the Growth Tier

## Pricing Tiers (per month, ex VAT, billed in ZAR)

- **Core Tier** - R 42,000 per month. Up to 25 users, 1 TB managed storage, 5 connectors.
- **Growth Tier** - R 88,000 per month. Up to 100 users, 5 TB managed storage, unlimited connectors.
- **Enterprise Tier** - R 175,000 per month. Unlimited users, 25 TB managed storage, dedicated data architect, custom SLAs.
