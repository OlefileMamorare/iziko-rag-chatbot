# Pieter Coetzee

## Role

Head of Customer Success, Iziko Cloud Solutions.

## Location

Cape Town, Western Cape.

## Background

Pieter joined Iziko in 2019 as the company's first Customer Success Manager and now leads a team of 7 CSMs across the country. He spent his early career in enterprise software sales at SAP South Africa. He holds a BCom (Marketing) from Stellenbosch University.

## Current Responsibilities

- Leads renewals, expansions, and customer health for all 24 enterprise accounts
- Owns the company-wide Net Revenue Retention (NRR) metric, currently at 118%
- Personally manages Iziko's two largest accounts: Highveld Energy Holdings and Sandton Capital Partners

## Compensation (2025)

- Base salary: R 1,320,000 per annum
- Short-term incentive: up to 20% of base, tied to NRR and gross retention
- Phantom equity: granted, vesting on the standard 4-year schedule

## Achievements

- Maintained gross logo retention above 96% for three consecutive years
- Designed the Iziko Customer Health Score, now used as the company's primary leading indicator
