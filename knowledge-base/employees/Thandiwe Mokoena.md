# Thandiwe Mokoena

## Role

Co-Founder and Chief Executive Officer (CEO), Iziko Cloud Solutions.

## Location

Cape Town, Western Cape.

## Background

Thandiwe founded Iziko Cloud Solutions in 2017 after a decade leading enterprise cloud transformation projects at a major South African telecommunications operator. She holds a BSc (Hons) in Computer Science from the University of Cape Town and an MBA from the UCT Graduate School of Business.

## Current Responsibilities

- Overall strategy and vision for Iziko
- Direct relationships with Iziko's top 5 enterprise accounts
- Chair of the Iziko Foundation, the company's CSI vehicle that funds coding bootcamps in the Western Cape

## Compensation (2025)

- Base salary: R 2,950,000 per annum
- Short-term incentive: up to 20% of base, paid against company-level OKRs
- Founder equity holding (not part of the phantom equity scheme)

## Achievements

- Listed on the Mail & Guardian Top 200 Young South Africans (Business category, 2022)
- Speaker at AfricaCom 2024 keynote on "Sovereign Cloud for African Enterprise"
