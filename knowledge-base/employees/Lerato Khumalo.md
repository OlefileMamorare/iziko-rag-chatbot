# Lerato Khumalo

## Role

Senior Product Manager, IzikoConnect.

## Location

Johannesburg, Gauteng.

## Background

Lerato joined Iziko in 2021 from a global SaaS company where she shipped collaboration features to over 12 million users. She holds a BCom (Hons) in Information Systems from the University of Pretoria.

## Current Responsibilities

- Owns the product roadmap and pricing for IzikoConnect
- Manages a squad of 6 engineers, 1 designer, and 1 data analyst
- Acts as the primary product contact for IzikoConnect's three largest customers: Cape Town Mutual Bank, Karoo Logistics Group, and Pioneer Retail South Africa

## Compensation (2025)

- Base salary: R 1,180,000 per annum
- Short-term incentive: up to 15% of base
- Phantom equity: granted, vesting on the standard 4-year schedule

## Notable Work

- Launched IzikoConnect's WhatsApp Business integration in 2023, now used by 80% of enterprise customers
- Drove the customer-research programme that informed the Enterprise Tier launch in 2024
