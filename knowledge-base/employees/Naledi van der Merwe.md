# Naledi van der Merwe

## Role

Chief Financial Officer (CFO), Iziko Cloud Solutions.

## Location

Cape Town, Western Cape.

## Background

Naledi joined Iziko in 2020 from a JSE-listed industrial holding company where she was Group Financial Controller. She is a qualified Chartered Accountant (CA(SA)) and completed her articles at PwC Cape Town.

## Current Responsibilities

- Owns finance, procurement, legal, and the company's POPIA and B-BBEE compliance posture
- Responsible for Iziko's annual external audit
- Co-leads commercial negotiations on enterprise contracts above R 1 million in annual contract value

## Compensation (2025)

- Base salary: R 1,720,000 per annum
- Short-term incentive: up to 15% of base
- Phantom equity: granted, vesting on the standard 4-year schedule

## Notable Work

- Led Iziko's successful Level 1 B-BBEE recertification in 2024
- Implemented the company-wide Rand-billing standard, which removed all USD pricing from new contracts in 2023
