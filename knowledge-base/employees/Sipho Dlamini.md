# Sipho Dlamini

## Role

Head of Engineering, Iziko Cloud Solutions.

## Location

Johannesburg, Gauteng.

## Background

Sipho joined Iziko in 2018 as the third engineering hire and now leads a team of 14 engineers across the four product lines. Before Iziko, he spent seven years at a Big Four consulting firm building security platforms for South African banks. He holds a BEng in Electrical & Computer Engineering from the University of the Witwatersrand.

## Current Responsibilities

- Owns the technical roadmap for IzikoGuard and is the executive sponsor for the IzikoGuard Detection Engine
- Manages engineering hiring, performance, and the graduate programme
- Reports directly to CEO Thandiwe Mokoena

## Compensation (2025)

- Base salary: R 1,850,000 per annum
- Short-term incentive: up to 15% of base
- Phantom equity: granted, vesting on the standard 4-year schedule

## Achievements

- Winner of the **2023 South African Tech Innovator of the Year (SATIOTY) award** for designing the IzikoGuard Detection Engine
- Holds two patents (jointly with Iziko) on threat-detection techniques for cloud workloads
