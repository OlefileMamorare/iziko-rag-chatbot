# Careers at Iziko Cloud Solutions

Iziko hires across engineering, product, customer success, sales, and operations. We currently employ 38 people across our Cape Town, Johannesburg, and Durban offices.

## Hiring Process

1. Application review (3-5 working days)
2. 30-minute introductory call with the People team
3. Role-specific technical or case interview (60-90 minutes)
4. Team-fit panel with 2-3 future colleagues
5. Final conversation with the hiring manager and one executive

All candidates receive feedback within 5 working days of each stage.

## Open Programmes

- **Iziko Graduate Programme** - a 12-month structured rotation for recent graduates across two product teams. Intake of 6 graduates each February.
- **Iziko Returnship** - a 6-month programme for senior engineers and PMs returning to the workforce after a career break (parental leave, caregiving, sabbatical).
- **Iziko Apprenticeship** - a 24-month learnership for school-leavers with a strong interest in cloud operations and cybersecurity. Two cohorts per year.

## Benefits Snapshot

- Competitive salaries benchmarked annually against the JCSE / OfferZen Rand reports.
- Annual short-term incentive bonus of up to 15% of base salary.
- Phantom equity scheme for all permanent employees after 12 months of service.
- 25 days annual leave plus public holidays.
- Discovery Health Keycare Plus medical cover (employee + immediate family).
- Monthly R600 connectivity allowance and a R15,000 once-off home-office stipend.
- R20,000 annual learning & conference budget per employee.

## How to Apply

Send a CV and short cover note to careers@iziko-cloud.co.za, mentioning the role title and the office you'd be based in.
