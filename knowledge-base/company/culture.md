# Culture at Iziko Cloud Solutions

Iziko's culture is built on the principle of _ubuntu_ - "I am because we are." We believe technology is built by people, for people, and that great software is the result of healthy, diverse, and collaborative teams.

## Operating Principles

1. **Customer Outcomes First** - Every feature, contract, and support ticket is measured by the outcome it delivers for the customer.
2. **Local Roots, Global Standards** - We build for South African realities (load-shedding, mobile-first users, data sovereignty) but hold ourselves to international quality benchmarks.
3. **Open by Default** - Salary bands, OKRs, and engineering decisions are documented and shared across the company.
4. **Sustainable Pace** - We design for the long run. Iziko has a four-day "deep work" Friday policy where meetings are discouraged.

## Hybrid Working

Iziko operates on a hybrid model. Employees are expected to be in their nearest office at least two days per week (typically Tuesday and Thursday), with the remaining days flexible. Every full-time employee receives a R15,000 once-off home-office stipend on joining and a R600 monthly connectivity allowance.

## Diversity & Inclusion

Iziko is a Level 1 B-BBEE contributor. The company runs a structured graduate programme that takes in 6 graduates per year from South African universities, with a deliberate focus on candidates from historically disadvantaged backgrounds.

## Wellbeing

- 25 days annual leave (in addition to South African public holidays)
- Two paid mental-health days per year
- Discovery Health Keycare Plus cover for all employees and immediate family
- Monthly "Iziko Hour" - a town-hall hosted by CEO Thandiwe Mokoena
