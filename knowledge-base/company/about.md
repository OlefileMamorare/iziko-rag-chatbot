# About Iziko Cloud Solutions

Iziko Cloud Solutions (Pty) Ltd was founded by Thandiwe Mokoena in 2017 in Cape Town as a South African cloud and IT services company. The name "Iziko" comes from the isiXhosa word for "hearth" or "central gathering place" - reflecting the company's mission to be the trusted technology hub for African enterprises.

The company started with a single product, IzikoConnect, a unified cloud collaboration platform built for organisations operating across South Africa's distributed workforce. After winning early contracts with mid-sized financial services firms in Sandton and Cape Town, Iziko grew rapidly between 2018 and 2021, opening satellite offices in Johannesburg, Durban, and Pretoria, and reaching a peak headcount of 180 employees by the end of 2021.

In 2022 the company underwent a strategic right-sizing programme, shifting to a hybrid-remote operating model and consolidating its Pretoria office into Johannesburg. As of 2025, Iziko operates with a focused team of 38 employees across three offices - Cape Town (head office), Johannesburg, and Durban - serving 24 active enterprise contracts spanning all four product lines.

Iziko's product portfolio has expanded from its original collaboration platform into four flagship offerings:

- **IzikoConnect** - cloud collaboration and unified communications platform (8 active contracts)
- **IzikoGuard** - managed cybersecurity and threat-detection service (7 active contracts)
- **IzikoData** - data analytics and business intelligence platform (5 active contracts)
- **IzikoFlow** - low-code workflow automation platform (4 active contracts)

The company's clients range from regional retailers and logistics operators to JSE-listed financial services groups and provincial government departments, demonstrating Iziko's ability to deliver across the South African enterprise market.
