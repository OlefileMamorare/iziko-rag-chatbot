# Company Overview

## Vision

Iziko Cloud Solutions exists to make enterprise-grade cloud, security, and automation technology accessible, affordable, and locally relevant for South African and broader African organisations.

## Mission

We help our customers modernise the way they work by combining global-class cloud platforms with deep local engineering and support, ensuring data sovereignty and POPIA compliance every step of the way.

## Key Facts

- **Founded:** 2017
- **Founder & CEO:** Thandiwe Mokoena
- **Headquarters:** Cape Town, South Africa
- **Offices:** Cape Town, Johannesburg, Durban
- **Employees (2025):** 38
- **Active contracts (2025):** 24
- **Product lines:** 4 (IzikoConnect, IzikoGuard, IzikoData, IzikoFlow)
- **Registration:** Iziko Cloud Solutions (Pty) Ltd, registered in South Africa
- **Compliance:** POPIA-compliant; ISO 27001 certified since 2021

## Awards & Recognition

- 2023 - South African Tech Innovator of the Year (SATIOTY) awarded to **Sipho Dlamini**, Head of Engineering, for his work on the IzikoGuard threat-detection engine.
- 2024 - JSE Top Empowering Tech SMEs (shortlisted).
- 2025 - AfricaCom "Best Cloud Service for SMEs" (IzikoConnect Standard Tier).

## Strategic Positioning

Iziko competes by offering global-grade tooling that is delivered, supported, and billed locally in Rand, with engineers based in the same time zone as the customer. Our differentiators are POPIA-first design, locally hosted data, and a high-touch customer success model.
